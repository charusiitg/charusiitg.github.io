IRCTC Railconnect Redesign

User Experience Design

Interaction Design

 

I redesigned and prototyped the primary ticket booking flow of the irctc app as a part of my assignment during my internship at Sapient Nitro.

 

To go through the redesign process I took remote interviews of users and expert reviews to carry on heuristic evaluation of the app to justify the reason the to redesign it. After justifying the purpose of the project I followed the traditional waterfall methodology following the agile UX approach. After laying out the guiding principles for the redesign Carried on with user research, making personas, user journey and information architecture. Then made the wireframes of the entire flow. Making wireframes was an iterative approach, I used to make a set of wireframes, gut it reviewed by discussing it in meetings and iterate it accordingly. Arrived on the final wireframes after 3-4 complete and many partial Iterations. Prototyped the flow on invision.
